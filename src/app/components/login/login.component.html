QR Sync

QR Sync is a QR code / barcode scanning application for Android.

It makes it easy to save QR codes you scan harnessing the power of cloud databases.

All of your codes are automatically kept in sync across all of your devices, so you can access them wherever you need them.

This website can be used to access and read your codes from your web browser.

To download the application and create an account, please visit our Google Play Store page.
